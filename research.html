Public preprint

When Does Removing LayerNorm Help? Activation Bounding as a Regime-Dependent Implicit Regularizer

This paper studies when replacing LayerNorm with bounded activations helps or hurts transformer training. The core finding is regime dependence: activation bounding can act like implicit regularization in low-data, overparameterized settings, while standard LayerNorm remains better in data-rich regimes.
